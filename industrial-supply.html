Engagement Models

How We Work With You

We adapt our supply model to match your procurement style, risk profile and internal processes.

Model 01

Project-Based Supply

Material supply linked to a specific project, with quantities and delivery schedule aligned to the project plan.

  • • BOQ-driven material list
  • • Phased deliveries as per work fronts
  • • Flexibility for variation orders
Model 02

Rate Contract

Pre-agreed rates for defined items over a period, with call-off orders issued as needed.

  • • Rate stability for a defined tenure
  • • Faster order processing
  • • Suitable for recurring requirements
Model 03

Annual Supply Agreement

Long-term supply partnership for multi-site clients, managed centrally with site-wise deliveries.

  • • Centralized commercial agreement
  • • Site-wise logistics planning
  • • Better predictability of cost & availability
