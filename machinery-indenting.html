How We Work

Indenting Workflow

A structured, transparent process designed to protect both client and supplier interests.

01

Requirement & BOQ Review

We begin by understanding your project, BOQ, performance specs, and site conditions to define the exact machinery configuration.

02

OEM Shortlisting & Techno-Commercial Offer

Shortlisting global OEMs, aligning technical specs, and preparing comparative techno-commercial quotations.

03

Negotiation & LC Arrangement

Finalizing pricing, delivery, and warranty terms, followed by LC opening and all related banking documentation.

04

Production, Shipment & Clearance

Tracking manufacturing, shipment schedules, and supervising customs clearance for timely delivery to your site.

05

Installation & After-Sales

Coordinating OEM engineers for installation, commissioning, and after-sales support where applicable.

*We can also support tender documentation (technical offer, data sheets, compliance statements) for government and large private projects where machinery packages are part of the scope.
