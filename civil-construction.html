How We Execute

Construction Delivery Process

A structured, milestone-based approach that keeps stakeholders aligned and projects on schedule.

01

Pre-Construction Planning

Review of drawings, specifications, BOQ and site constraints. Development of work program, resource plans and method statements.

02

Mobilization & Site Setup

Deployment of site team, setting up temporary facilities, safety controls, and survey benchmarks.

03

Core Civil Execution

Execution of foundations, structural works, road layers and finishes under supervision of qualified engineers.

04

Quality & Inspections

Regular testing, documentation, and joint inspections with client/consultant teams.

05

Handover & Closeout

Snag rectification, as-built documentation, and formal handover of completed works.

*We can participate as primary contractor or as a specialized civil sub-contractor depending on project size and structure.

Compliance & HSE

We treat safety, compliance, and quality as non-negotiables across every site we operate.

Material testing for concrete, aggregates and other critical materials as per standards.
